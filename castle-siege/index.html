📜 How to Play

Goal

Endless waves march toward your castle gate. Each enemy that slips through costs lives (bosses cost 10!). Survive as long as you can.

Towers (tab 1)

  • 9 types — pierce armor with magic (❄🔥⚡☠), shred flesh with arrows and cannonballs.
  • Click a built tower to upgrade (5 levels, looks & hits harder) or sell.
  • Gold Mint makes passive income; Holy Beacon buffs nearby towers.

Army (tab 2)

  • 12 troop types unlock as waves pass. They block and fight enemies on the road.
  • Set each type's keep-count — the game auto-resummons them when they fall.
  • Upgrade each type (⬆) to make every future summon stronger.

Hero (tab 3)

Sir Aldric fights from wave 1, cleaves crowds, and slams packs. Train him with gold; move him with H.

Enemies

14+ types — armored foes resist physical damage (use magic!). Watch for glowing Elites and Champions. Every 10th wave brings a Boss.

Multitask mode 😎

Turn AUTO waves on, set your army loadout, build some Gold Mints, crank speed to 3×… and check back between meetings. Progress autosaves each wave.

Keys

1–9 build • H move hero • R rally point • Space pause • F speed • Esc cancel
